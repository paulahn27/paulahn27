Project Brief

Objective

Allow anyone to shop, transfer money, and more without a debit or credit card or the need to visit a physical bank or store.

Context

The world of buying and selling has changed—more and more transactions happen online without the need for a physical credit or debit card. The amount of online sales and transactions is and has been steadily increasing, consumers are subscribing to a multitude of online services, and high street stores are closing in large numbers.

There are many factors that make online purchases favorable, from cost and convenience, to security, to name but a few. This ecommerce trend has been catalyzed by the global coronavirus pandemic in 2020, forcing many people to stay inside, wary of going to the store and of using their physical cards to pay for items.

With it, this trend has brought forward the problem of how to make purchases securely and efficiently. How can your banking and card details be stored in one place? How can this same tool be used to make transactions or transfers in online stores and overseas? What’s important to users when buying online?

Possible Problems

Security: Some users will feel like there is a possibility that their account will get hacked and someone else will have access to their banking information

International Purchases: Making purchases overseas, users could be skeptical of the reliability and security of certain stores.

Organization: For users that have a plethora of different credit and debit cards, they may want a feature that allows them to store all of their cards in an organized and efficient manner.

Budgeting: It is easy for some users to get caught up in online shopping and spend more than they anticipated. These people would like to reduce their amount of monthly excess spending.

Problem Statement

Our PlutoPay app users need a way to quickly and effectively transfer money to different accounts, use that money to buy their favorite products online, and budget their monthly income. Most importantly, our users should feel safe and secured while managing their account. We will know this to be true when we see that people are consistently using the app and making transactions that they would normally do in person. Our retention rate should stay high because users are interested in what is offered.

Chime

Overview

The amount of subtle yet effective features that Chime offers makes it a unique app and a strong competitor. As advertised on their website, they offer no overdraft fees up to $100, no minimum balance requirements, no foreign transaction fees, and no transfer fees. They also demonstrate a high interest in security measures as all purchases are protected through Visa’s Zero-Liability Policy and deposit insurance up to $250,000 through Member FDIC. All of these features indicate Chime’s attempt to give opportunities to their users to budget and handle their money properly no matter what circumstance they’re in.

SWOT Analysis

Strengths

  • Popular in the iOS and Andriod app store, 5 million downloads
  • All features in the app are included, no additional fee to access certain parts
  • Offers unique features such as SpotMe and Early Direct Deposit
  • Security through Visa Zero Liability Policy

Weaknesses

  • Customer service needs improvement
  • Process for applying for a Visa debit card could be a hassle
  • APY offered is about the same as other competitors

Oppurtunities

  • More search features so that users can access a certain part of the app as quick as possible
  • Add budgeting feature so that users don't always have to use SpotMe
  • Include feature that allows users to socially connect with others while on the app.

Threats

  • The market for online banking apps is fairly large because there are banking companies with and without physical banks
  • With everything being virtual it may be harder to have a more personal connections with clients
  • Connecting accounts with multiple banks could be a hassle

Simple

Overview

Simple separates themselves from other competitors by offering a free, high yield checking account that automatically accumulates interest no matter how long or how often you use the account. They present themselves as convenient and secure, naming their most advertised feature “Protected Goals Account.” In essence, their focus is making sure that their users are reaching the budgeting and financing goals, highlighting their above average APY and expense organization.

SWOT Analysis

Strengths

  • Popular in the iOS and Andriod app store, 500k+ downloads
  • Protected Goals Account, to help users feel confident about reaching their goals
  • High yield checking account, competitive against other apps
  • As advertised, makes budgeting as simple as possible

Weaknesses

  • Customer service needs improvement, slow response time
  • Process for applying for a Visa debit card could be a hassle
  • APY offered is about the same as other competitors
  • Design could use improvement, a lot of information is clustered together

Oppurtunities

  • More search features so that users can access a certain part of the app as quick as possible
  • Offer expert advice or a way to connect with others that are comfortable with banking
  • Add news section so users are able to see news related to the policies that Simple supports

Threats

  • Branding could be difficult if you aren't active on certain social media websites while other competitors are.
  • With everything being virtual it may be harder to have a more personal connections with clients
  • It may be hard to keep the interface for web and mobile similar and well-functioning
